Common Questions

Straight Answers.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts, no lock-in. You can pause, change your plan, or cancel anytime with 48 hours notice before your next scheduled visit.

What if I need to reschedule a visit?

Just reach out at least 24 hours in advance and we'll move your appointment to a time that works. Subscription clients get scheduling priority.

Can I add services to a subscription visit?

Absolutely. You can add on any à la carte service at a discounted rate for subscribers. Just let us know when you book or when we arrive.

Do prices change based on vehicle size?

Yes — use the vehicle size toggle at the top of this page to see pricing for your specific vehicle type. Trucks and large SUVs are priced slightly higher.

How do I pay?

We accept all major credit cards and payment apps. You'll be billed per visit after each appointment is completed — no prepayment required.

Can I have multiple vehicles on one plan?

Yes — multi-vehicle households get a discount on each additional vehicle added to a plan. Reach out to us directly to set that up.
